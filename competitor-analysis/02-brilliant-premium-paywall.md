# Brilliant — Premium / Paywall Screen

## Overview
A paywall/upsell modal comparing Free vs. Premium features in a clean comparison table. The app is **Brilliant**.

---

## Layout & Structure
- **Full-screen modal** with a close (×) button in the top-right corner
- **Hero headline**: large, centered, multi-line text at the top
- **Comparison table**: two columns (Free vs. Premium), rows for each feature
- **CTA button**: full-width at the bottom
- **No tab bar visible** — this is a modal overlay

### Hierarchy
1. Headline with "Premium" keyword highlighted
2. Comparison table with feature rows
3. CTA button at the bottom

---

## Spacing & Padding
- **Top padding**: generous (~40–48px from status bar to headline)
- **Headline to table**: ~32–40px gap
- **Table row height**: ~56–64px per row, comfortable touch targets
- **Row separators**: subtle horizontal lines between rows
- **CTA button**: full-width, ~16px horizontal margin, ~48–52px height
- **Bottom padding**: generous spacing below CTA to screen edge

---

## Color Usage
- **Background**: soft warm gradient at the top (peach/pink tones fading to white), white for the main content area
- **"Premium" text**: gradient text effect — purple to orange/gold, eye-catching
- **Premium column**: multi-color gradient border/background (blue → purple → pink → yellow), very vibrant
- **Free column**: neutral, no background treatment
- **Checkmarks (Free)**: green outlined checkmark on light green circle — small, subdued
- **Checkmarks (Premium)**: orange/amber checkmark on light circle — larger, more prominent
- **X marks (Free)**: gray × on light gray circle — indicates missing features
- **CTA button**: dark charcoal/near-black with white text, rounded
- **Close button**: gray × icon

---

## Typography
- **Headline**: large (~26–30px), bold, black, centered, multi-line
- **"Premium" word**: same size but with gradient color treatment — purple-to-gold
- **Column headers**: "Free" in regular gray, "Premium" in bold dark on gradient background
- **Feature labels**: medium (~15–16px), regular weight, left-aligned, dark text
- **"Benefits" label**: medium (~14px), bold, left-aligned
- **CTA text**: medium (~16px), bold, white, centered
- **Font**: clean sans-serif, likely same system as the rest of the app

---

## Patterns & Components
- **Gradient hero area**: warm peach/pink gradient at the top creates a premium, inviting feel
- **Gradient text**: "Premium" uses a gradient fill — a modern, premium design pattern
- **Comparison table**: standard Free vs. Premium feature matrix, very common in SaaS/subscription apps
- **Gradient border on Premium column**: rainbow/multi-color gradient border makes the premium option visually dominant
- **Icon states**: ✓ (included) vs. × (not included) with color coding
- **Premium column visual weight**: significantly more decorated than Free — draws the eye
- **Full-width dark CTA**: "Start my free week" — action-oriented, low-commitment copy
- **Close button**: simple × in the corner, unobtrusive

---

## Design Principles Observed
1. **Visual hierarchy favors Premium**: the gradient border, larger check icons, and color treatment all make Premium the obvious choice
2. **Anchoring**: showing what Free users lack (× marks) creates a sense of missing out
3. **Warm, inviting palette**: the peach gradient feels friendly and non-aggressive for a paywall
4. **Minimal friction**: "Start my free week" is low-commitment CTA copy
5. **Contrast-driven decision making**: stark visual difference between Free (plain) and Premium (colorful) columns
6. **Clean data presentation**: simple table layout makes comparison effortless
